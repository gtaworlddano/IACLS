PACIFIC COAST CONCRETE

Industrial-Grade Concrete Production & Placement across San Andreas

About Us

Pacific Coast Concrete is a construction materials and contracting company specializing in the production, transport, and placement of industrial-grade concrete across San Andreas.

Our operations support municipal infrastructure, commercial development, and residential construction projects requiring reliable, large-scale concrete supply and skilled placement crews.

While Pacific Coast Concrete does not operate a quarry directly, we plan to maintain a standing supply agreement with Davis Quartz Mining Facility, operated by Caldera Minerals, where our crews plan to source raw materials used in our batching and concrete production process.

Through this partnership, Pacific Coast Concrete gathers and transports essential aggregate materials from the quarry before processing them for construction use across the region.

Our crews regularly coordinate with state agencies, private contractors, and government project managers to complete roadway pours, structural foundations, industrial slabs, and other infrastructure work throughout Blaine County and surrounding areas.

Services Provided

Pacific Coast Concrete offers full concrete supply and install services, including:

  • Concrete Mixing & Delivery
  • Commercial & Residential Concrete Placement
  • Structural Foundations & Slab Pours
  • Roadway & Infrastructure Construction
  • Aggregate Transport & Material Supply
  • Government Contracting & Public Works Projects

Projects range from small residential pours to large municipal construction contracts requiring coordinated crews and heavy equipment.

Operations Process

Material Collection: Crews travel to Davis Quartz Mining Facility to load aggregate and raw materials used in our concrete mixtures.

Transport & Staging: Materials are transported to designated staging areas or directly to project sites.

Concrete Placement: Work crews prepare forms, mix materials, and perform concrete pours using finishing and placement equipment.

Finishing & Quality Assurance: Surfaces are leveled, finished, and cured according to project specifications and construction standards.
