Irish-American Club of Los Santos

"The Irish-American Club of Los Santos believes that strong communities begin with strong youth programs. Through educational, cultural, and leadership initiatives, we aim to provide opportunities for young residents across San Andreas."

About Us

The Irish-American Club of Los Santos (IACLS) is a community-focused non-profit organization dedicated to celebrating Irish heritage, supporting local families, and strengthening the cultural fabric of Los Santos.

Founded in 1987 by Patrick O'Donnell, Michael Fitzgerald, and a small group of first- and second-generation Irish-American residents, the club was established during a period of rapid growth throughout Los Santos. Many Irish families had settled throughout neighborhoods such as East Vinewood, Rockford Hills, and Del Perro, yet lacked a centralized organization dedicated to preserving their cultural traditions and fostering community involvement.

What began as a handful of families gathering in rented community halls soon evolved into a formally chartered organization focused on cultural preservation, charitable outreach, youth development, and civic engagement.

Today, the IACLS serves as a gathering place for residents from all backgrounds who share an appreciation for Irish culture and a commitment to community service. Through educational programs, charitable initiatives, cultural events, and partnerships with local businesses, the club continues to honor the values upon which it was founded: Community, Heritage, Charity, Education, & Service.

Youth Programs

We firmly believe that investing in the next generation is the key to a thriving Los Santos. Our programs include:

Youth Leadership Program

A mentorship program connecting local professionals with young adults interested in public service, business, and community leadership.

Scholarship Fund

Annual educational scholarships awarded to graduating high school students who demonstrate academic achievement, community involvement, and leadership potential.

Cultural Education Workshops

Programs designed to teach Irish history, traditions, music, and cultural heritage in an engaging and accessible environment.

Community Service Initiative

Volunteer opportunities allowing youth members to contribute to food drives, neighborhood improvement projects, charity fundraisers, and civic events.

Athletic & Recreation Programs

Seasonal recreational activities that encourage teamwork, sportsmanship, and healthy lifestyles.

Future Leaders Summer Program

A summer development program focused on leadership skills, public speaking, community engagement, volunteer service, and career exploration.

Club Executives

The Irish-American Club of Los Santos is governed by a volunteer Board of Directors responsible for overseeing the organization's operations, financial stewardship, community initiatives, and long-term strategic direction. The Board is elected by the membership and serves as the primary governing body of the Club.
